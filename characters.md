# Characters

In Last Odyssey, [characters](/extras/key-terms#term-character) are like JRPG heroes, with strong personalities and detailed mechanics for how they function in combat.  The following section lays out what goes into defining a character in Last Odyssey, and how to create characters for your campaign.

* [Character Creation:](/characters/creation) An overview of the elements of character building.
* [Archetypes:](/characters/archetypes) Unique sets of abilities that define each character.
* [Jobs:](/characters/jobs) A character's proficiencies during battle.
* [Attributes and Statistics:](/characters/attributes-and-statistics) The mechanical heart of every character.
* [Eidolons:](/characters/eidolons) The expressions of a character's Anima made manifest.
* [Character Advancement:](/characters/advancement) How characters grow stronger.
* [Character Sheet:](/characters/last-odyssey-fillable-character-sheet.pdf) Form-fillable character sheet.
* [Sample Characters:](/characters/examples) Ready-made characters for your campaign.
