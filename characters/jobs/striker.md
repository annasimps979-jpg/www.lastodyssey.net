# Striker

_<dfn id = "term-striker">Striker</dfn>_ specializes in [physical attacks](/combat-rules/attacking-and-defending#term-physical-attack).

While they are Strikers, [player characters](/extras/key-terms#term-player-character) gain the following bonuses to their [statistics](/extras/key-terms#term-statistic):
*  Their [Physical Attack](/characters/attributes-and-statistics#term-physical-attack) is increased by an amount equal to their [Body](/characters/attributes-and-statistics#term-body)
*  Their [Evasion](/characters/attributes-and-statistics#term-evasion) is increased by an amount equal to their [Mind](/characters/attributes-and-statistics#term-mind).
*  Their [Physical Defense](/characters/attributes-and-statistics#term-physical-defense) is increased by an amount equal to their [Spirit](/characters/attributes-and-statistics#term-spirit).
