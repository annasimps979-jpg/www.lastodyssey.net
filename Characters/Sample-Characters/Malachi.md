# Malachi

**Concept:** A Salamander from the Rikki Jungle.  When his village came down with a mysterious illness, he decided to leave home in order to find a cure.

**Aspiration:** Save his village from destruction.

**Relationships:** Love for his Children, Distrust of Humans, Anger at the Gods

**Class:** Ranger

**Elemental Type:** Wood (Innate)
