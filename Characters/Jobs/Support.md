# Support

Supports specialize in buffs, Shells, and beneficial status effects.

While they are Supports, [player characters](../../Extras/Key-Terms#term-player-character) gain the following bonuses to their [statistics](../../Extras/Key-Terms#term-statistic):
*  Their [Physical Defense](../../Characters/Attributes-and-Statistics#term-physical-defense) is increased by an amount equal to their [Body](../../Characters/Attributes-and-Statistics#term-body).
*  Their [Magical Defense](../../Characters/Attributes-and-Statistics#term-magical-defense) is increased by an amount equal to their [Mind](../../Characters/Attributes-and-Statistics#term-mind).
*  Their [Magical Attack](../../Characters/Attributes-and-Statistics#term-magical-attack) is increased by an amount equal to their [Spirit](../../Characters/Attributes-and-Statistics#term-spirit).
