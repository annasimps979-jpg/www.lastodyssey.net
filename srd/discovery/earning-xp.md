The progress of the [Heroes](/srd/glossary#term-hero) through a [campaign](/srd/glossary#term-campaign) is measured by the amount of _<dfn id = "term-xp">experience</dfn>_ they earn.  Experience, or 🜹 for short, is a resource that can be spent to make characters stronger and more capable.  See the [Hero Advancement](/srd/heroes/advancement) section for more details.  In total, it takes 1,155 🜹 to progress a character from the beginning of [tier](/srd/heroes/creation#term-tier) 1 to the end of tier 10.  Planning out the relative amount of 🜹 that [players](/srd/glossary#term-player) will earn from different sources is one way for the [GM](/srd/glossary#term-gm) to determine what elements of a campaign to emphasize.  

## Varieties of 🜹

Canonically, there are five different ways that Heroes can earn 🜹:
*  _<dfn id = "term-combat-xp">Combat 🜹</dfn>_ is earned by defeating [warriors](/srd/warriors#term-warrior) and [Antagonists](/srd/anima/antagonists) in [battle](/srd/glossary#term-battle).  Defeating a [Normal](/srd/warriors/creation#term-normal-warrior) warrior in [combat](/srd/combat) is worth 1 🜹, defeating an [Elite](/srd/warriors/creation#term-elite-warrior) warrior is worth 3 🜹, and defeating an Antagonist is worth 5 🜹 per [Form](/srd/anima/antagonists/defeat#term-form) defeated, adjusted for the relative tier of the players and their opponents.
*  _<dfn id = "term-exploration-xp">Exploration 🜹</dfn>_ is earned by discovering new and interesting places in the [overworld](/srd/exploration#term-overworld).  Coming across a [landmark](/srd/exploration/settlements/landmarks), arriving at a new, easily accessible [node](/srd/exploration/maps#term-node), discovering a new level of a [dungeon](/srd/exploration/dungeons), and overcoming a [natural hazard](/srd/exploration/encounters#term-natural-hazard) are all worth 1 🜹, while discovering a hard-to-reach node or secret area is worth 5 🜹.
*  _<dfn id = "term-lore-xp">Lore 🜹</dfn>_ is earned by learning and recording previously unknown or actionable knowledge about the world.  Each small piece of [Lore](/srd/discovery/lore) is worth 1 🜹, each secret or larger chunk of Lore is worth 5 🜹, and campaign-defining revelations are worth 10 🜹 each.
*  _<dfn id = "term-puzzle-xp">Puzzle 🜹</dfn>_ is earned each time players successfully solve a stage of a [puzzle](/srd/discovery/puzzles).  Each stage of a puzzle is worth 5 additional 🜹.
*  _<dfn id = "term-quest-xp">Quest 🜹</dfn>_ is earned when Heroes advance the plot of a campaign.  Each [stage](/srd/discovery/quests#term-quest-stage) of a [quest](/srd/discovery/quests#term-quest) the players complete is worth 5 🜹, while completing a whole quest that is more than two stages long is worth an additional 10 🜹.

## Always Add, Never Exclude

Sometimes, players will earn multiple kinds of 🜹 for doing the same thing, particularly in the case of quest 🜹.  If this is the case, they will earn all relevant kinds of 🜹.  So, for example, if the [party](/srd/glossary#term-party) defeats a [Villain](/srd/anima/antagonists/villains) and concludes a quest as a result, then they will earn the quest 🜹 for completing the quest and the combat 🜹 for winning in combat.
