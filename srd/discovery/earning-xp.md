The progress of the [Heroes](/srd/glossary#term-hero) through a [campaign](/srd/glossary#term-campaign) is measured by the amount of _<dfn id = "term-xp">experience</dfn>_ they earn.  Experience, or <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr> for short, is a resource that can be spent to make characters stronger and more capable.  See the [Hero Advancement](/srd/heroes/advancement) section for more details.  In total, it takes 1,155 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr> to progress a character from the beginning of [tier](/srd/heroes/creation#term-tier) 1 to the end of tier 10.  Planning out the relative amount of <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr> that [players](/srd/glossary#term-player) will earn from different sources is one way for the <abbr title="game master">[GM](/srd/glossary#term-gm)</abbr> to determine what elements of a campaign to emphasize.  

## Varieties of <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>

Canonically, there are five different ways that Heroes can earn <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>:
*  _<dfn id = "term-combat-xp">Combat <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr></dfn>_ is earned by defeating [warriors](/srd/warriors#term-warrior) and [Antagonists](/srd/anima/antagonists) in [battle](/srd/glossary#term-battle).  Defeating a [Normal](/srd/warriors/creation#term-normal-warrior) warrior in [combat](/srd/combat) is worth 1 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>, defeating an [Elite](/srd/warriors/creation#term-elite-warrior) warrior is worth 3 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>, and defeating an Antagonist is worth 5 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr> per [Form](/srd/anima/antagonists/defeat#term-form) defeated, adjusted for the relative tier of the players and their opponents.
*  _<dfn id = "term-exploration-xp">Exploration <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr></dfn>_ is earned by discovering new and interesting places in the [overworld](/srd/exploration#term-overworld).  Coming across a [landmark](/srd/exploration/settlements/landmarks), arriving at a new, easily accessible [node](/srd/exploration/maps#term-node), discovering a new level of a [dungeon](/srd/exploration/dungeons), and overcoming a [natural hazard](/srd/exploration/encounters#term-natural-hazard) are all worth 1 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>, while discovering a hard-to-reach node or secret area is worth 5 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>.
*  _<dfn id = "term-lore-xp">Lore <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr></dfn>_ is earned by learning and recording previously unknown or actionable knowledge about the world.  Each small piece of [Lore](/srd/discovery/lore) is worth 1 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>, each secret or larger chunk of Lore is worth 5 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>, and campaign-defining revelations are worth 10 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr> each.
*  _<dfn id = "term-puzzle-xp">Puzzle <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr></dfn>_ is earned each time players successfully solve a stage of a [puzzle](/srd/discovery/puzzles).  Each stage of a puzzle is worth 5 additional <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>.
*  _<dfn id = "term-quest-xp">Quest <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr></dfn>_ is earned when Heroes advance the plot of a campaign.  Each [stage](/srd/discovery/quests#term-quest-stage) of a [quest](/srd/discovery/quests#term-quest) the players complete is worth 5 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>, while completing a whole quest that is more than two stages long is worth an additional 10 <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>.

## Always Add, Never Exclude

Sometimes, players will earn multiple kinds of <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr> for doing the same thing, particularly in the case of quest <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>.  If this is the case, they will earn all relevant kinds of <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>.  So, for example, if the [party](/srd/glossary#term-party) defeats a [Villain](/srd/anima/antagonists/villains) and concludes a quest as a result, then they will earn the quest <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr> for completing the quest and the combat <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr> for winning in combat.
