# Travel

*Or, there and back again.*

As the [party](/srd/glossary#term-party) crosses the [overworld](/srd/exploration#term-overworld), they will expend
resources to do so.  Which resources and how many depend on their means of travel.  In the
core ruleset, there are two main ways that they may do this: on foot, in which case their journey is represented by the travel round, and by using travel services such as merchant caravans or vehicles.

- On Foot
    - [The Travel Round](/srd/exploration/travel/round): The rules for foot travel, and the dangers involved.
- By Vehicle
    - [Travel Services](/srd/exploration/travel/services): Caravans, airships, landships, and the like, and how the party may make use of them.
