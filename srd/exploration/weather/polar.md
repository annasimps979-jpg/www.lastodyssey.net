# Polar

The farthest reaches of the poles and glaciers and cold regions during the winter resemble the Polar climate.  It is also useful for representing the peak of a tall mountain range.

**Example [Natural Hazards](/srd/exploration/encounters#term-natural-hazard):** fragile ice, a slippery slope, a deep snowbank.

## Weather Table

|Result|Weather|
|:-|:-|
|1-3|[Bright](/srd/exploration/weather/effects#term-bright)|
|4-7|[Snowy](/srd/exploration/weather/effects#term-snowy)|
|8-10|[Snowstorm](/srd/exploration/weather/storms#term-snowstorm)|

Due to the cold, [Heroes](/srd/glossary#term-hero) in Polar regions should always be wearing a Coldproof [Cloak](/srd/economy/items/travel#term-cloak) or piece of [armor](/srd/economy/equipment/armor).  [Players](/srd/glossary#term-player) whose [characters](/srd/glossary#term-character) are not will cast the [outcome die](/srd/glossary#term-outcome-die) during each [Camping Phase](/srd/exploration/travel/round#term-camping-phase) regardless of the weather.  Each character whose player receives an 8 or above will develop the [Frostbitten](/srd/combat/statuses#term-frostbitten) [status](/srd/combat/statuses).  In Polar regions, the [navigation difficulty](/srd/exploration/maps#term-navigation-difficulty) of a path is 5 greater than it otherwise would be due to the effects of snow blindness, ice, snow, and the shifting of the ground.
