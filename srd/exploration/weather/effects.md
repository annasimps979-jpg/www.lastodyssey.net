# Weather Effects

Each variety of weather comes with its own unique effects and possible dangers.  In order of severity, the possible weather effects are shown in the table below.

|Weather|Effect|
|:-|:-|
|<dfn id = "term-sunny">Sunny</dfn>|The sky is a pleasant, clear blue, and the temperature is mild.  The players do not experience any penalties or bonuses.|
|<dfn id = "term-cloudy">Cloudy</dfn>|The sky is filled with clouds that cast shadows over the ground, and the temperature is cool.  The players do not experience any bonuses or penalties.|
|<dfn id = "term-bright">Bright</dfn>|The sun is out and beating down right on the [characters](/srd/key-terms#term-character).  At the end of the [Camping Phase](/srd/exploration/travel/round#term-camping-phase), all characters that did not wear a Sunproof [Cloak](/srd/economy/items/travel#term-cloak) or piece of [armor](/srd/economy/equipment/armor) will receive the [Burned](/srd/combat/statuses#term-burned) [status](/srd/combat/statuses).  During [combat](/srd/combat) [encounters](/srd/enemies/encounters#term-encounter), all characters who are not wearing a Sunproof Cloak will begin the first [round](/srd/combat/round#term-round) with the [Blinded](/srd/combat/statuses#term-blinded) status.|
|<dfn id = "term-rainy">Rainy</dfn>|Rain is coming down from the clouds, making tools and equipment damp.  All characters that did not wear a Waterproof Cloak or piece of armor will lose one [item](/srd/economy/items) of the GM's choice due to their pack filling with water.  On Rainy days, the [navigation difficulty](/srd/exploration/nodes-and-paths#term-navigation-difficulty) of the path counts as 1 greater than normal.|
|<dfn id = "term-foggy">Foggy</dfn>|A fine, white mist cloaks everything, making it significantly harder to see.  On Foggy days, the navigation difficulty of the path counts as 1 greater than normal.  In addition, enemies during enemy encounters will gain [First Strike](/srd/combat/starting#term-first-strike) unless the [Scout](/srd/exploration/travel/round#term-scout-action) [action](/srd/key-terms#term-action) was used the day before.|
|<dfn id = "term-snowy">Snowy</dfn>|Snow drifts gently down from the sky, piling up on the ground and creating problems for the unwary.  Players whose characters that did not wear a Coldproof Cloak or piece of armor should cast 1d4 during the Camping Phase.  Each character whose player receives a 4 will develop the [Frostbitten](/srd/combat/statuses#term-frostbitten) status.|
|<dfn id = "term-hot">Hot</dfn>|It is unbelievably hot, far hotter than the human body can truly handle.  At the end of the Camping Phase, every player whose character did not wear a Heatproof Cloak or piece of armor should cast 1d4.  If the result is a 1, their character takes an [injury](/srd/combat/death-and-injury#term-injury) as though they had been [downed](/srd/combat/death-and-injury#term-downed) in [battle](/srd/key-terms#term-battle).|
|<dfn id = "term-humid">Humid</dfn>|It should be raining, but it isn't, and it's so hot and sticky out that it's difficult to breathe.  At the end of the Camping Phase, all characters that did not wear a Waterproof Cloak will receive the [Poisoned](/srd/combat/statuses#term-poisoned) status.  Characters that do not wear a Heatproof Cloak should cast 1d4.  If the result is a 1, their character takes an injury as though they had been downed in battle.|
