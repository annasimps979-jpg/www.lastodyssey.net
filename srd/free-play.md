# Free Play

One of the cornerstones of FRP is _free play,_ a state of the game in which the [actions](/srd/extras/key-terms#term-action) [players](/srd/extras/key-terms#term-player) can take are not strictly delineated by the rules for system such as [combat](/srd/combat) or the [travel round](/srd/exploration/travel/round), but rather determined by the context of the narrative and by the rulings made by the [GM](/srd/extras/key-terms#term-gm).  The following section is a guide to the mechanics and frameworks that exist in Last Odyssey that GMs can use to facilitate free play while following Last Odyssey's mechanical and storytelling principles.

*  [Resting:](/srd/free-play/resting) The ways in which players can recover after combat and travel.
*  [Skill Challenges:](/srd/free-play/skill-challenges) When [player characters](/srd/extras/key-terms#term-player-character) attempt to use their innate capabilities during free play.
*  [Investigation:](/srd/free-play/investigation) Learning new information about the world and about the [party's](/srd/extras/key-terms#term-party) enemies.
*  [Abilities Outside of Combat:](/srd/free-play/abilities-outside-combat) Tips for making rulings about the use of combat powers during free play.
*  [Anima:](/srd/free-play/anima) How to earn and spend Anima, which gives players an edge over their opponents and is necessary for advancing in tier.
*  [True Death:](/srd/free-play/true-death) What happens when [characters](/srd/extras/key-terms#term-character) really die instead of being [downed](/srd/combat/death-and-injury#term-downed).
*  [Earning XP:](/srd/free-play/earning-xp) The means by which characters grow stronger within a single tier.
*  [Lore:](/srd/free-play/lore) A guide to actionable knowledge, and how players might acquire it.
*  [Puzzles:](/srd/free-play/puzzles) Tips and advice for adding puzzle encounters to [campaigns](/srd/extras/key-terms#term-campaign).
*  [Quests:](/srd/free-play/quests) XP-generating tasks that guide players through a campaign.
