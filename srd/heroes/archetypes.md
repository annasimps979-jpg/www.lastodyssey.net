# Archetypes

*I am I am I*

Every [Hero](/srd/key-terms#term-hero) has a single _<dfn id = "term-archetype">archetype</dfn>_ that represents the qualities that make them heroic, with each one being most associated with a single heroic virtue.  At [Hero creation](/srd/heroes/creation), a Hero's archetype determines their [armor](/srd/economy/equipment/armor) and [weapon](/srd/economy/equipment/weapons) [proficiencies](/srd/heroes/creation#term-weapon-proficiency).  It also determines the [statistic](/srd/key-terms#term-statistic) that their [buff](/srd/combat/buffs-and-debuffs#term-buff) [abilities](/srd/key-terms#term-ability) in the [Support](/srd/heroes/roles/support) [role](/srd/heroes/roles) increase, as well as the [statistic](/srd/key-terms#term-statistic) that their [debuff](/srd/combat/buffs-and-debuffs#term-debuff) abilities in the [Saboteur](/srd/heroes/roles/saboteur) role decrease, and gives a bonus to the Hero's [Health](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-health), [Mana](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-mana), and [Initiative](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-initiative).  As [players](/srd/key-terms#term-player) increase their Heroes' [tier](/srd/heroes/creation#term-tier), their archetypes will also provide abilities that can be used regardless of what role you currently play in [combat](/srd/combat).  Additionally, archetypes provide talents to choose from which modify their existing abilities while they play the associated role.  At tier 10, instead of unlocking a normal combat ability, players instead unlock a capstone ability that their Heroes' [eidolons](/srd/heroes/eidolons#term-eidolon) can use when summoned.

- The Nine Archetypes
    - [The Ranger](/srd/heroes/archetypes/ranger): An archetype with extra mobility and physical precision.
    - [The Thief](/srd/heroes/archetypes/thief): An archetype with the ability to steal and hide in order to manipulate the flow of [battle](/srd/key-terms#term-battle).
    - [The Artificer](/srd/heroes/archetypes/artificer): An archetype that can make special use of [battle items](/srd/economy/items/battle) and learn information about their enemies.
    - [The Sage](/srd/heroes/archetypes/sage): An archetype that specializes in defending against and dealing magical damage.
    - [The Dancer](/srd/heroes/archetypes/dancer): An archetype that can mimic enemies and change their [elemental type](/srd/combat/elemental-types#term-elemental-type) at will.
    - [The Priest](/srd/heroes/archetypes/priest): An archetype with the power to heal [MP](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-mp) and help their allies.
    - [The Knight](/srd/heroes/archetypes/knight): An archetype that focuses on dealing and taking physical damage.
    - [The Commander](/srd/heroes/archetypes/commander): An archetype that can help augment their teammates' capabilities.
    - [The Harlequin](/srd/heroes/archetypes/harlequin): An archetype that uses random abilities to throw their enemies off balance.
