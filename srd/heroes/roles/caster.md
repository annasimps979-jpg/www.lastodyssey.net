# Caster

_<dfn id = "term-caster">Casters</dfn>_ specialize in [magical attacks](/srd/combat/attacking-and-defending#term-magical-attack).

While they are Casters, [Heroes](/srd/key-terms#term-hero) gain the following bonuses to their [statistics](/srd/key-terms#term-statistic):

*  Their [Resistance](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-resistance) is increased by an amount equal to their [Body](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-body).
*  Their [Magical Attack](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-magical-attack) is increased by an amount equal to their [Mind](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-mind).
*  Their [Magical Defense](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-magical-defense) is increased by an amount equal to their [Spirit](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-spirit).
