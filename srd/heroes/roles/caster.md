# Caster

_<dfn id = "term-caster">Casters</dfn>_ specialize in [spells](/srd/combat/attacks#term-spell).

While they are Casters, [Heroes](/srd/glossary#term-hero) gain the following bonuses to their [statistics](/srd/glossary#term-statistic):

*  Their [Resistance](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-resistance) is increased by an amount equal to their [Body](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-body).
*  Their [Magical Attack](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-spell) is increased by an amount equal to their [Mind](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-mind).
*  Their [Magical Defense](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-magical-defense) is increased by an amount equal to their [Spirit](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-spirit).
