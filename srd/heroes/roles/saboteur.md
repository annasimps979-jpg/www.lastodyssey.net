The Saboteur specializes in applying [hexes](/srd/combat/glamors#term-hex) and negative [statuses](/srd/combat/statuses).

While they are Saboteurs, [Heroes](/srd/glossary#term-hero) gain the following bonuses to their [statistics](/srd/glossary#term-statistic):
*  Their [Resistance](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-resistance) is increased by an amount equal to their [Body](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-body).
*  Their [Physical Attack](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-strike) is increased by an amount equal to their [Mind](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-mind).
*  Their [Evasion](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-evasion) is increased by an amount equal to their [Spirit](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-spirit).
