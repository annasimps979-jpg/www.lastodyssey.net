# Striker

_<dfn id = "term-striker">Strikers</dfn>_ specialize in [physical attacks](/srd/combat/attacks#term-physical-attack).

While they are Strikers, [Heroes](/srd/key-terms#term-hero) gain the following bonuses to their [statistics](/srd/key-terms#term-statistic):
*  Their [Physical Attack](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-physical-attack) is increased by an amount equal to their [Body](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-body)
*  Their [Evasion](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-evasion) is increased by an amount equal to their [Mind](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-mind).
*  Their [Physical Defense](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-physical-defense) is increased by an amount equal to their [Spirit](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-spirit).
