# Defender

_<dfn id = "term-defender">Defenders</dfn>_ specialize in drawing and negating the effects of enemy attacks.

While they are Defenders, [Heroes](/srd/key-terms#term-hero) gain the following bonuses to their [statistics](/srd/key-terms#term-statistic):
*  Their [Physical Defense](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-physical-defense) is increased by their [Body](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-body).
*  Their [Resistance](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-resistance) is increased by their [Mind](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-mind).
*  Their [Magical Defense](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-magical-defense) is increased by their [Spirit](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-spirit).

## Abilities by Rank

|Rank|Name|[Type](/srd/combat/attacks#term-ability-type)|Cost|Description|
|:-|:-|:-|:-|:-|
|1|Taunt|[Ranged](/srd/combat/attacks#term-ranged)|1 [AP](/srd/combat/round#term-ap)|The Defender inflicts the Taunted [status](/srd/combat/statuses) on a single target until the beginning of their next turn, not counting held turns.  A Taunted [character](/srd/key-terms#term-character) can only use single-target [magical attacks](/srd/combat/attacks#term-magical-attack), [physical attacks](/srd/combat/attacks#term-physical-attack), and [status-inflicting](/srd/combat/attacks#term-status-inflicting-ability) [abilities](/srd/key-terms#term-ability), and only on the character who inflicted it.  It can also use the [Move](/srd/combat/round#term-move) [action](/srd/key-terms#term-action) to move into range of their target if needed.  Each character can only be Taunted by a single other character at a time.  If a Taunted character cannot reach the Defender by any means, they will act as though they are not Taunted.|
|2|Guard|[Ally](/srd/combat/attacks#term-ally-ability)|5 [MP](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-mp), 1 AP|The Defender inflicts the Guarded status on a single ally, which remains until the beginning of the Defender's next [turn](/srd/combat/round#term-turn), not counting held turns.  Every time that ally is targeted by a physical attack, magical attack, or status-inflicting ability, the Defender becomes the target instead.  If a Guarded ally is targeted by a Multi- or All-type ability, the ability is simply negated against them.|
|3|Shield|[Self](/srd/combat/attacks#term-self-ability)|7 MP, 1 AP|The Defender inflicts the Shielded status on themselves.  While a character is Shielded, if they are hit by a physical attack the damage dealt to them is automatically reduced to 0 and the Shielded status effect is removed.|
|4|Counter|Self|14 MP, 1 AP|The Defender inflicts the Countering status effect on themselves until the beginning of their next turn.  While a character is Countering, when they are hit by a physical attack the attacker takes damage as though they were also a target of the same attack.|
|5|Multitaunt|[Multi](/srd/combat/attacks#term-multi)|9 MP, 1 AP|The Defender inflicts the Taunted status on every enemy on the battlefield until the beginning of the Defender's next turn, not counting held turns.|
|6|Multiguard|Multi|10 MP, 1 AP|The Defender inflicts the Guarded status on all of their allies until their next turn, not counting held turns.|
|7|Barrier|Self|18 MP, 1 AP|The Defender inflicts the Barred status effect on themselves.  While a character is Barred, if they are hit by a magical attack the damage dealt to them is automatically reduced to 0 and the Barred status effect is removed.|
|8|Reflect|Self|24 MP, 1 AP|The Defender inflicts the Reflecting status effect on themselves until the end of their next turn.  While a character is Reflecting, the next time they are hit with a magical attack the attacker takes damage as though they were also a target of the same attack.|
|9|Deathless|[Passive](/srd/combat/attacks#term-passive)|N/A|When the Defender is hit with an attack that would otherwise [down](/srd/combat/death-and-injury#term-downed) them while at anything but 1 [HP](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-hp) and 0 [Shell](/srd/combat/shells), they are instead reduced to 1 HP and 0 Shell.|
|10|Mirror Shield|Self|1 [Anima](/srd/anima#term-anima), 2 AP|The [eidolon](/srd/heroes/eidolons#term-eidolon) inflicts the Mirrored status on itself, which goes away at the beginning of its next turn.  While the eidolon is Mirrored, all magical attacks, physical attacks, and status-inflicting abilities that hit it are negated and reflected back onto the enemy that used them.|
