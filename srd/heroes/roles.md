# Roles

*The masks one wears onstage.*

The _<dfn id = "term-role">role</dfn>_ a Hero plays determines the part they play in [combat](/srd/combat).  Each Hero may invest in any and all of the available roles in Last Odyssey, but may only play one role at a time.  Out of combat, they may switch between roles at will, while in combat they must switch roles during the [Paradigm Phase](/srd/combat/round#term-paradigm-phase).  While they play a role, they may use any of its associated abilities that their player has unlocked via [advancement](/srd/heroes/advancement) except for the capstone [ability](/srd/glossary#term-ability), which can only be used by their [eidolon](/srd/heroes/eidolons#term-eidolon).  Any of the unlocked [passive](/srd/combat/attacks#term-passive) abilities for a role, such as Trained Spellcaster, only apply if the Hero currently occupies the role.  In addition, while playing a given role a Hero gains extra role-dependent bonuses to three different [statistics](/srd/glossary#term-statistic) equal to their three [attributes](/srd/glossary#term-attribute).

- The Six Roles
    - [Striker:](/srd/heroes/roles/striker) An offensive role focused on doing damage to the [front row](/srd/combat/starting#term-front-row).
    - [Caster:](/srd/heroes/roles/caster) An offensive role focused on doing damage to the entire battlefield.
    - [Healer:](/srd/heroes/roles/healer) A defensive role focused on healing and removing [statuses](/srd/combat/statuses) from allies.
    - [Defender:](/srd/heroes/roles/defender) A defensive role focused on drawing warrior attacks and negating received damage.
    - [Support:](/srd/heroes/roles/support) A defensive role focused on [charming](/srd/combat/glamors#term-charm) allies and protecting them from [elemental](/srd/combat/elements#term-element) damage.
    - [Saboteur:](/srd/heroes/roles/saboteur) An offensive role focused on [hexing](/srd/combat/glamors#term-hex) warriors and making them vulnerable to elemental damage.
