# Jobs

The _<dfn id = "term-job">job</dfn>_ a character currently occupies determines their role in [combat](/srd/combat).  Each character may invest in any and all of the available jobs in Last Odyssey, but may only occupy one job at a time.  Out of combat, they may switch between jobs at will, while in combat they must switch jobs during the [Paradigm Phase](/srd/combat/round#term-paradigm-phase).  While they occupy a job, they may use any of the abilities that you have unlocked via [character advancement](/srd/characters/advancement) except for the capstone [ability](/srd/key-terms#term-ability), which can only be used by their [eidolon](/srd/characters/eidolons#term-eidolon).  Any of the unlocked [passive](/srd/combat/attacking-and-defending#term-passive) abilities for a job, such as Trained Spellcaster, only apply if the character currently occupies the job.  In addition, they gain extra bonuses to three different [statistics](/srd/key-terms#term-statistic) equal to their three [attributes](/srd/key-terms#term-attribute).

In Last Odyssey, there are six distinct jobs: Striker, Caster, Healer, Defender, Support, and Saboteur.
*  [Striker:](/srd/characters/jobs/striker) An offensive job focused on doing damage to the [front row](/srd/combat/starting#term-front-row).
*  [Caster:](/srd/characters/jobs/caster) An offensive job focused on doing damage to the entire battlefield.
*  [Healer:](/srd/characters/jobs/healer) A defensive job focused on healing and removing [statuses](/srd/combat/statuses) from allies.
*  [Defender:](/srd/characters/jobs/defender) A defensive job focused on drawing enemy attacks and negating received damage.
*  [Support:](/srd/characters/jobs/support) A defensive job focused on [buffing](/srd/combat/buffs-and-debuffs#term-buff) allies and protecting them from [elemental](/srd/combat/elemental-types#term-elemental-type) damage.
*  [Saboteur:](/srd/characters/jobs/saboteur) An offensive job focused on [debuffing](/srd/combat/buffs-and-debuffs#term-debuff) enemies and making them vulnerable to elemental damage.
