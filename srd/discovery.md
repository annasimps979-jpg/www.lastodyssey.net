*What if I don't know what will happen?*

There will often be times at which the [actions](/srd/glossary#term-action) [players](/srd/glossary#term-player) can take are not strictly delineated by the rules for system such as [combat](/srd/combat) or the [travel round](/srd/exploration/travel/round), but rather determined by the context of the narrative and by the rulings made by the <abbr title="game master">[GM](/srd/glossary#term-gm)</abbr>.  In Last Odyssey, this type of play is referred to as _<dfn id = "term-discovery">discovery</dfn>._  The following section is a guide to the mechanics and frameworks that exist in Last Odyssey that <abbr title="game master">GM</abbr>s can use to facilitate discovery while following Last Odyssey's mechanical and storytelling principles.

- How to Learn
    - [Investigation:](/srd/discovery/investigation) Learning new information about the world and about the [party's](/srd/glossary#term-party) adversaries.
    - [Lore:](/srd/discovery/lore) A guide to actionable knowledge, and how players might acquire it.
- How to Strive
    - [Puzzles:](/srd/discovery/puzzles) Tips and advice for adding puzzle encounters to [campaigns](/srd/glossary#term-campaign).
    - [Trials:](/srd/discovery/trials) When [Heroes](/srd/glossary#term-hero) attempt to use their innate capabilities during discovery.
    - [Magic:](/srd/discovery/magic) Tips for making rulings about the use of combat abilities during discovery.
- How to Grow
    - [Earning Experience:](/srd/discovery/earning-xp) The means by which characters grow stronger within a single tier.
    - [Quests:](/srd/discovery/quests) <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>-generating tasks that guide players through a campaign.
