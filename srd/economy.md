# The Economy

*As for the living: only some may think, but all must eat.*

In Last Odyssey, the [party](/srd/glossary#term-party) will have to rely on goods and services received from [NPCs](/srd/glossary#term-npc) in order survive during [exploration](/srd/exploration) and [combat](/srd/combat).  Acquiring these these things can be a matter of completing [quests,](/srd/discovery/quests) stealing, borrowing, and receiving gifts from strangers, but is most often a matter of participating in the in-game economy.

- Acquisition
    - [Currency:](/srd/economy/currency) The basic unit of exchange for all goods and services, and how players can acquire it.
    - [Merchants:](/srd/economy/merchants) Item vendors and how to create them.
    - [Merchant Sheet:](/srd/economy/last-odyssey-fillable-merchant-sheet.pdf) A place to record the items a vendor carries.
- Inventory
    - [Inventory:](/srd/economy/inventory) What can be carried, and how much.
    - [Equipment:](/srd/economy/equipment) Weapons, armor, and accessories.
    - [Items:](/srd/economy/items) Battle items, travel items, and miscellaneous carried objects.
