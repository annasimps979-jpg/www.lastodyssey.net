Some items that [players](/srd/glossary#term-player) can purchase do not have utility in [battle](/srd/glossary#term-battle) but instead during [exploration](/srd/exploration).  In Last Odyssey, these are referred to as _travel items._  

## Supplies

The most basic variety of travel items is _<dfn id = "term-supplies">Supplies</dfn>,_ which represent the mundane objects a [Hero](/srd/glossary#term-hero) might need while on the road, such as torches, a lanterns, rope, or sets of climbing gear.  A [character](/srd/glossary#term-character) may expend 1-3 units of Supplies at any time to create a new object in their [inventory](/srd/economy/inventory#term-inventory).  This piece of gear will stay in their inventory and take up an amount of [Carrying Capacity](/srd/economy/items#term-carrying-capacity) equal to the number of Supplies expended to create it, and will last for about as long as it would in real life.  For example, a torch will burn out after a night of use, a lantern after several nights, and climbing gear will stay in a Hero's inventory until they take a bad fall or remove it.  Supplies cost 1 [<abbr title = "currency">¤</abbr>](/srd/economy/currency#term-cr) per unit when bought from a [merchant](/srd/economy/merchants), and each unit of Supplies or item equivalent to a unit of Supplies has an [item rating](/srd/economy/items#term-item-rating) of 1.  The amount of Supplies that an item requires will depend on its size and rarity:
*  Small items, such as flint, tinder, or kindling, cost 0 Supplies.
*  Hand-sized items such as lanterns or torches cost 1 Supplies.
*  Medium-sized items or items stored in bulk such as lantern fuel or rope cost 2 Supplies.
*  Large items or items that must be carried outside of a pack such as ladders or tents cost 3 Supplies.
*  Items that are larger than a one-person canoe cannot be created with Supplies, and must be bought instead.

Novice maps reduce the navigation difficulty by 1, Standard by 3, and Expert by 7.|

_<dfn id = "term-scroll">Scrolls</dfn>_ are single-use items that allow Heroes to use extra actions during the [Camping Phase](/srd/exploration/travel/round#term-camping-phase) that cause effects during the next day.  Each Scroll has an item rating of 1.  Scrolls may also be used during [discovery,](/srd/discovery#term-discovery) in which case they will take 6 in-game hours to fully activate.
