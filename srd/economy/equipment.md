# Equipment

*A hammer is a hand, and a hand is a hammer.*

A [character's](/srd/key-terms#term-character) [base](/srd/key-terms#term-base) [Physical](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-strike) and [Magical Attack](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-spell) and [Physical](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-physical-defense) and [Magical Defense](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-magical-defense) as well as their base [Evasion](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-evasion) and [Resistance](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-resistance) are affected by special items that they carry on their person into [combat](/srd/combat) called _<dfn id = "term-equipment">equipment</dfn>._  All [Heroes](/srd/key-terms#term-hero) can wear three types of equipment: weapons, which give bonuses to their base Magical and Physical Attack; armor, which gives bonuses to their base Physical and Magical Defense; and accessories, which give bonuses to their base Evasion and Resistance.

The potency of a piece of equipment is determined by its _<dfn id = "term-equipment-quality">Quality</dfn>._  There are four levels of Quality a piece of gear can have: Mundane, Rare, Expert, and Unique.  The first three levels give flat increases to the bonuses the respective piece of armor or weaponry provides to a character's [statistics](/srd/key-terms#term-statistic).  

- What to Hold
    - [Weapons:](/srd/economy/equipment/weapons) Tools of violence that contribute greatly to a Hero's base damage.
- What to Wear
    - [Armor:](/srd/economy/equipment/armor) Special clothing that contributes greatly to a Hero's base defenses.
    - [Accessories:](/srd/economy/equipment/accessories) Extra wearables that contribute to a Hero's ability to ward off danger.
- How to Modify It
    - [Equipment Upgrades:](/srd/economy/equipment/upgrades) Extras that give equipment its character; all sorts of effects can be added.
