---
title: Defeating Antagonists

permalink: /srd/anima/antagonists/defeat

abstract: >-
    The rules for defeating Antagonists.
---

The power of Antagonists is reflected by their ability to accumulate and use Anima.  Like [Heroes](/srd/glossary#term-hero), an Antagonist can have a maximum of 10 Anima at a time.  During [battle](/srd/glossary#term-battle), Antagonists are not defeated when they are [downed](/srd/combat/injuries#term-downed).  Instead, each time they are downed, they will change into their next Form during the [Paradigm Phase](/srd/combat/round#term-paradigm-phase) until they have run out.  Each _<dfn id = "term-form">Form</dfn>_ of an Antagonist corresponds to each subsequent set of [statistics and abilities](/srd/heroes/numbers) they are able to employ during battle.  Once an Antagonist has run out of Forms and cannot use the Revive [action](/srd/glossary#term-action), they will then be permanently defeated.

After an Antagonist is defeated, players will earn 100 [cr](/srd/economy/currency#term-cr) multiplied by a Form's [tier](/srd/heroes/creation#term-tier) per defeated Form as well as 1-4 pieces of [equipment](/srd/economy/equipment).  If the Antagonist has any unused Anima remaining when they are finally defeated, it dissipates, and cannot be reclaimed.  Antagonists can have as many Forms as the [GM](/srd/glossary#term-gm) wants, but they are balanced for each battle to be against one or two in a row.  Defeating an Antagonist in [combat](/srd/combat) is also worth 5 [XP](/srd/discovery/earning-xp#term-xp) per Form defeated multiplied the tier of the Form.  So, defeating an Antagonist that has one Form at tier 2 and another at tier 4 would be worth 10 XP plus 20 XP for a total of 30 XP.
