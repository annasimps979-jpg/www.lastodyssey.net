Each [Hero](/srd/glossary#term-hero) is empowered with magic whose root is the [<abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr>](/srd/anima) accumulated inside of their souls.  All Heroes can have a maximum of 10 <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> at a time.  Once they accumulate this much, they must spend some in order to earn any more.  

The purpose of <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> is to manipulate fate in the Heroes' favor beyond what would be possible through ordinary means.  As such, players can spend <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> in or out of [combat](/srd/combat) in any of the following ways:
*  During travel, they may spend 1 <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> to automatically succeed when [pathfinding](/srd/exploration/travel/round#term-pathfinding).
*  In combat, they can spend 3 <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> to take the [Summon Eidolon](/srd/combat/round#term-summon-eidolon) [action](/srd/glossary#term-action).
*  In combat, they must spend 1 <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> per round to keep their eidolon on the field.
*  In combat, they may spend 1 <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> to turn any die result into either a 1 or a 10, with the exception of [calling Initiative](/srd/combat/starting#term-calling-initiative).
*  In and out of combat, they can spend 3 <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> to save themselves from True Death.  If so, they will be revived at 1 [HP](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-hp) during the [Paradigm Phase](/srd/combat/round#term-paradigm-phase) of the following round, or after a making contact with their eidolon if they are not in combat.  They must do this at the moment of their own death, and they cannot intervene to save any other characters but themselves.  If a Hero saves themselves from True Death after dying due to a [Scene](/srd/anima/scenes), no <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> is earned as a result of the Scene.
*  Out of combat, they can spend 1 <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> to automatically succeed on a trial.
*  Out of combat, they can spend 1 <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> to ask whether or not the situation they are in is dangerous.
*  Out of combat, they can spend 1 <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> to revive their eidolon to full HP.

## The Consequences of <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> Usage

Every time any character, Hero or otherwise, uses <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> and it is not transferred to a character that can already use <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr>, the <abbr title="game master">GM</abbr> add how much was used to a running tally called the _<dfn id = "term-anima-pool"><abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> Pool</dfn>_ that is hidden from all participants except the <abbr title="game master">GM</abbr>.  When Heroes earn <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> from [Scenes,](/srd/anima/scenes) this score drops by the amount that was earned.  When the <abbr title="game master">GM</abbr> creates a new Antagonist, they may give them starting <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> that is then removed from this tally, up to the character maximum of 10.  No matter what, the <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> Pool can never go below 0.  Note that the <abbr title="game master">GM</abbr> still has the freedom to introduce Antagonists whenever they feel is appropriate.

During combat with an Antagonist, any <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> spent on one side is immediately gained by the opposing side.  Any excess <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> gained above 10 dissipates instead.
