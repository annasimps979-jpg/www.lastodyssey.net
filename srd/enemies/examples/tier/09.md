# Tier 9 Enemies

## Invaders
Alternate dimensions contain sentients of their own.  While most transdimensional visitors are benign or even benevolent, those that willingly choose to colonize other dimensions often aren't.

### Lore

* Invaders don't follow too many rules in [combat](/srd/combat), except for one: do whatever the [players](/srd/glossary#term-player) expect the least at any given time.

## Robots
Advanced artificial minds often enact their will via numerous servitors, some of which are also equipped for battle.

### Lore

* In battle, robots are part of a single hive mind.  Likewise, they behave as though they are being coordinated by an intelligent mind that sees them merely as pawns to be sacrificed.
