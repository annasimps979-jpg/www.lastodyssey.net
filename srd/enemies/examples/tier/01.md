# Tier 1 Enemies

## Police
The police are deployed against protestors, activists, criminals, and anyone else that the government perceives as threatening the established order.

### Lore

* In [combat](/srd/combat), police will swap between [rounds](/srd/combat/round#term-round) when they use their [debuffs](/srd/combat/buffs-and-debuffs#term-debuff) and rounds where they exert maximum force.  
* During the first round of [battle](/srd/key-terms#term-battle), police will not attack but instead sit there and either inflict [statuses](/srd/combat/statuses), use [buffs](/srd/combat/buffs-and-debuffs#term-buff) and debuffs, and generally look menacing.

## Goblins
Goblins are sentient woodland creatures that have their own language and culture.  During lean times, they will sometimes resort to raiding human settlements for food and supplies.

### Lore

* In combat, goblins are both bullies and cowards.  They will gang up on the weakest [party](/srd/key-terms#term-party) member they can attack, and will retreat to the [back row](/srd/combat/starting#term-back-row) as soon as they lose their shields.  
* Goblins are wicked and cunning, and smart enough to exploit magical and physical vulnerabilities (although they do not know the elements of their enemies off the bat).
