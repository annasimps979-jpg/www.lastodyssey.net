# Anima

In Last Odyssey, the [Heroes](/srd/key-terms#term-hero) are able to wield the power of magic.  Run-of-the-mill [abilities](/srd/key-terms#term-ability) are powered by [Mana](/srd/heroes/attributes-and-statistics#term-mana), but the most potent form of magic is instead powered by _<dfn id = "term-anima">Anima</dfn>,_ which manipulates fate in a way that only Heroes and [Antagonists](/srd/anima/antagonists) can achieve.  In order to summon their [eidolon](/srd/heroes/eidolons#term-eidolon) and to use certain abilities, [characters](/srd/key-terms#term-character) must spend Anima.  Anima finds its expression through the actions of the PCs and their [eidolons](/srd/heroes/eidolons), and in the existence of [Villains](/srd/anima/antagonists/villains) and [Monsters](/srd/anima/antagonists/monsters).  Anima is not good or bad, but simply powerful.  There is no way to destroy Anima without also destroying fate itself.
