*Catch fate with a lure, and let it slip out of your hands again.*

In Last Odyssey, the [Heroes](/srd/glossary#term-hero) are able to wield the power of magic.  Run-of-the-mill [abilities](/srd/glossary#term-ability) are powered by [Mana](/srd/heroes/numbers#term-mana), but the most potent form of magic is instead powered by _<dfn id = "term-anima">Anima</dfn>,_ which manipulates fate in a way that only Heroes and [Antagonists](/srd/anima/antagonists) can achieve.  Throughout this text, you will see the word "Anima" represented by the symbol <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr>.  In order to summon their [eidolon](/srd/heroes/eidolons#term-eidolon) and to use certain abilities, [characters](/srd/glossary#term-character) must spend <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr>.  <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> finds its expression through the actions of the <abbr title = "player character">PC</abbr>s and their [eidolons](/srd/heroes/eidolons), and in the existence of [Villains](/srd/anima/antagonists/villains) and [Monsters](/srd/anima/antagonists/monsters).  <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> is not good or bad, but simply powerful.  There is no way to destroy <abbr title = "anima">🜉</abbr> without also destroying life itself.
