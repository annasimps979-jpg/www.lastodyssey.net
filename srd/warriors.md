*Violence can be difficult to handle.  Only some have the ability to do it.*

In Last Odyssey, every [character](/srd/glossary#term-character) that is capable of fighting the [Heroes](/srd/glossary#term-hero) in [combat](/srd/combat) is called a _<dfn id = "term-warrior">warrior</dfn>._  Not every [<abbr title = "non-player character">NPC</abbr>](/srd/glossary#term-npc) is a warrior, but any <abbr title = "non-player character">NPC</abbr>s that also have a set of corresponding combat [statistics](/srd/glossary#term-statistic) and give the Heroes [<abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr>](/srd/heroes/advancement#term-experience-points) when they are defeated in combat.

A warrior's description will list their [tier](/srd/heroes/creation#term-tier), their creature type, their element, and whether they are Normal, Elite, or an Antagonist for the purposes of <abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr> calculation.  The header for every monster will look like this: ((<abbr title = "experience">🜹</abbr> Type) (Tier), (Creature Type), (Element)).  This will then be followed by their combat statistics and their abilities.
