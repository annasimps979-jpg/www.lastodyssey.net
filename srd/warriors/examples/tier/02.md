## Forest Dwellers
The deep woods are home to creatures that will defend their territory to the death, although they will also grant boons to travelers they trust.

### Lore

* Forest Dwellers are interested in murdering their opponents with as little fanfare as possible.  The exception to this is that they will prioritize survival in [combat](/srd/combat) over a quicker end to things.  
* Forest Dwellers will choose to use healing [abilities](/srd/glossary#term-ability) even when it isn't strictly needed, and playing it safe with [charms](/srd/combat/glamors#term-charm) and [hexes](/srd/combat/glamors#term-hex).

## Dwarves
Dwarves are the enigmatic dwellers of the deep caves.  They are friendly enough folk, but are possessive of their territory and their treasure, and will fight to the death to preserve the former and to acquire more of the latter.

### Lore

*   In combat, Dwarves are highly coordinated opponents, with each one focusing on their own niche in [battle](/srd/glossary#term-battle).  
*   Dwarves will employ smart tactics, and will not show concern for injury except when it threatens their life.
